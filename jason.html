🔐 Key Tips to Protect Your Smart Home

  • Use strong and unique passwords for all smart devices.
  • Enable automatic updates to keep your devices secure.
  • Create a separate Wi-Fi network for smart home gadgets.
  • Use two-factor authentication whenever possible.
  • Review privacy settings regularly and limit data sharing.
